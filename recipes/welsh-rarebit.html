Welsh Rarebit

Description

Welsh Rarebit has nothing to do with rabbit, or potentially even Wales (careful), but is instead a simple, tasty recipe that is essentially posh cheese on toast

If you're going to follow the recipe properly, then you are going to need to put beer in it (does that make it Welsh?), but this is a breakfast, brucnh or lunch recipe and who's cracking open a tinnie at 10am? The Welsh?

Personally, I make it with a splash of milk, rather than beer, which still makes it taste beautiful. Don't skimp on the cheese, or even the bread, as this simple dish is all about high-quality ingredients.
